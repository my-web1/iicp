IICP's  Certificate - Master’s Program in Business Administration 

Program Overview

The Master’s Program in Business Administration at the International Institute of Career Progression (IICP) is a flexible, advanced management degree for ambitious graduates and experienced professionals. The program cultivates strategic thinking, specialist expertise, and practical leadership—empowering graduates to thrive in a range of modern business careers.

Why Choose IICP’s Master’s Program in Business Administration?

  • Contemporary Curriculum: Built to address real-world business challenges and industry needs.
  • Wide Range of Specializations: Choose from Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations, Hospital Management, BPO, and more.
  • Flexible Learning Paths: Study via fast-track (6 months), regular (1 year), or online modes.
  • Affordable & Transparent Fees: Accessible fee structure for Indian and international students.
  • Career-oriented Focus: Placement support, practical learning, and mentorship by experts.

Eligibility

  • Educational Qualification: Graduate degree or DBM (with minimum 2 years work experience).
  • Ideal For:

    • Fresh graduates aspiring for management roles.
    • Aspiring entrepreneurs.
    • STEM and social science graduates aiming to expand skill sets.

Admission Process

  1. Review eligibility and course details.
  2. Select your preferred mode and specialization.
  3. Prepare and submit documents:
    • Graduation/Diploma certificate (self-attested)
    • 3 passport-size photographs
    • Photo ID and address proof (self-attested)
  4. Complete the application (IICP advisors can assist as needed).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is prior work experience required?
Not required for all pathways—but preferred for fast-track options.
Can the MBA be pursued while working full time?
Yes. The program is suitable for working professionals and offers flexible formats.
What placement support does IICP provide?
IICP offers industry-linked placement assistance and career resources.
Is the MBA recognized?
The IICP MBA is well-regarded for its industry-oriented approach and practical value.
What documents are needed for admission?
Graduation/Diploma certificate (self-attested), 3 passport-size photos, and self-attested photo & address ID.
