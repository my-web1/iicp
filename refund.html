Refund & Cancellation Policy

Industry Integrated Career Platform is a private professional-training provider only. All fees are for skill-enhancement programmes, not for UGC/AICTE degrees or diplomas.

72-Hour Cooling-Off

Full refund if you cancel within 72 hours of payment and have not logged into the learning portal.

Pro-Rata Refund

If ≤ 10 % of digital content is accessed, you may claim a refund minus 10 % administrative charge.

No Refund Thereafter

Once more than 10 % of the course is consumed, or after 30 days from enrolment, no refund is permissible.

GST & Processing

Refunds are processed excluding GST paid to the government; credit to original payment mode within 10 working days.

Dispute resolution is governed by the Consumer Protection Act, 2019. Jurisdiction: Courts at pcmc, Maharashtra only.
