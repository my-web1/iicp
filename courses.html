Master Program in Business Administration

Overview: A two-year, industry-aligned certification training program designed to provide comprehensive training in business management with diverse specializations.

Post Graduate Diploma in Labour Laws (PGDLL)

Overview: One-year training and certification program covering industrial disputes, wages, social security, employee rights, trade unions, and workplace safety.

  • Duration: 1 year

PGTND

Post Graduate Program in Training and Development (PGTND)

Overview: Comprehensive program in instructional design, training delivery, and organizational learning. Ideal for HR professionals, corporate trainers, and freelance educators.

  • Eligibility: Graduate in any discipline or equivalent
  • Duration: 1 year (2 semesters of 6 months each, plus 3 months practical/project work)
  • Curriculum: 10 subjects covering training needs analysis, instructional design, training delivery, evaluation, organizational learning
  • Assessment: Semester exams, assignments, practical projects
