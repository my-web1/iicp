Flexible Exam Modes

Online Examinations: Register, download question papers, write answers on A4 paper, and upload as a single PDF. Email backup allowed in case of technical issues.
Case Study & Project-Based: Submit practical solutions for real-world scenarios, emphasizing application over rote learning.
Proctored Online Exams: Certain exams include webcam/audio proctoring for integrity.

Registration & Scheduling

Register for each session via the IICP portal. Exams are quarterly, with flexible scheduling for working professionals and multiple time zones. Choose your dates and formats during registration.

Exam Instructions & Integrity

Download papers at the scheduled time, write answers legibly on A4, and upload a single PDF in the portal. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited. All submissions are confidential and handled securely.

Transparent Evaluation

Qualified faculty, clear marking, and online results

Examination Process at IICP

At IICP, our examination process is designed for flexibility, transparency, and accessibility—ensuring that every student can demonstrate their learning effectively, whether through traditional or online modes.

Evaluation & Results

Faculty use clear, pre-defined marking criteria. Results are announced online and via email. Recheck or review requests are welcome.

Confidentiality & Integrity

All submissions and results are confidential. Open-book and case-based formats promote genuine learning and discourage malpractice.

Support & Resources

Receive detailed guidelines, marking schemes, and support via email and WhatsApp for any technical or procedural issues.

  • Accessible: Participate from anywhere—no center visits required.
  • Flexible: Exams fit your schedule within set windows.
  • Transparent: Clear marking and result communication.
  • Practical: Real-world application, not just theory.

The IICP examination process upholds academic standards while supporting diverse learner needs, ensuring every student has a fair and effective assessment experience.
