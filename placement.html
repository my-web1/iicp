Placement  Support

Empowering Your Career Journey

At IICP, we are dedicated to supporting your professional growth with a robust, technology-driven placement assistance program. Our approach combines real-time job alerts, curated learning resources, and personalized support to help you succeed in today’s job market.

More Details

Job Alerts via WhatsApp

Receive instant job notifications tailored to your skills and interests directly on WhatsApp and other platforms.

Aptitude & Technical Tests

Access a curated library of online aptitude and technical test resources, including practice questions and tutorials.

Soft Skills Development

Enhance your communication, teamwork, and interpersonal skills with selected videos and downloadable study notes.

Resume & CV Builder

Build professional, ATS-ready resumes using modern templates and expert guidance from online tools and guides.

Interview Q&A Videos

Prepare for technical and HR interviews with dedicated playlists and concise study notes for confident answers.

Online Interview Prep

Master virtual interviews with tutorials on body language, self-introduction, and online etiquette.

Extensive Online Support

Get guidance and answers to your queries via WhatsApp and email, plus access to a vast library of career resources.

Curated Learning Resources

Utilize a collection of high-quality videos, articles, and downloadable notes for self-paced learning.

Career Progress Tracker

Monitor your learning and job application progress with online tools and receive career tips from experts.

How to Get Started

Register for placement support, activate WhatsApp job alerts, access learning resources via your student portal, and connect with our team for personalized guidance.

At IICP, we offer autonomous, certification-based programs designed to meet the evolving needs of today’s professionals. Upon successful completion, learners are awarded Certificates that emphasize practical skills, industry relevance, and career advancement. While our certifications are not equivalent to university degrees or AICTE/UGC-recognized diplomas, they are highly valued in the private sector for their real-world application and flexible learning model
