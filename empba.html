"Executive -Master’s Program in Business Administration "

Program Overview

The Executive Master’s Program in Business Administration at the International Institute of Career Progression (IICP) is designed for mid-career professionals, managers, and leaders seeking to accelerate their career growth, sharpen their management competencies, and expand strategic business acumen. The program emphasizes practical knowledge, real-world leadership, and advanced management skills tailored for executive success.

Why Choose IICP’s Executive MBA?

  • Applied, Executive-Focused Curriculum: Addressing senior-level business challenges and leadership requirements.
  • Wide Range of Executive Specializations: Options such as Strategic Management, Finance, Marketing, HR, Operations, Healthcare, and more.
  • Flexible Learning Paths: Complete your Executive MBA via fast-track (6 months), regular (1 year), or online formats compatible with executive schedules.
  • Transparent, Competitive Fees: Value-driven fee structure for both Indian and international executives.
  • Career Impact: Enhanced placement visibility, executive networking opportunities, and mentorship by industry experts.

Eligibility

  • Educational Qualification: Graduate degree or DBM (with a minimum of 2 years of relevant work experience).
  • Who Should Apply:
    • Mid-career managers and business leaders aiming for C-suite or senior roles.
    • Entrepreneurs, business owners, and professionals seeking to broaden business leadership skills.
    • Functional managers in areas like operations, marketing, HR, or finance preparing for strategic roles.

Admission Process

  1. Review program and executive eligibility details.
  2. Choose your executive program mode and specialization.
  3. Prepare documents:
    • Graduate/Diploma certificate (self-attested)
    • 3 passport-size photographs
    • Photo ID and address proof (self-attested)
  4. Submit your completed application (IICP advisors available for support).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is prior managerial experience required?
At least 2 years of relevant professional experience is preferred, especially for fast-track or executive cohorts.
Can the Executive MBA be completed while working full time?
Yes. The format is designed for busy professionals and executives, with online or weekend classes and flexible pacing.
What kind of career progression can I expect?
Enhanced leadership readiness for promotions, transitions to senior management, and greater visibility in the executive talent pool.
Does IICP provide executive career support?
Yes, including network events, mentorship, and career guidance aligned with executive growth.
Which documents are compulsory for application?
Graduate/Diploma certificate (self-attested), 3 passport-size images, and valid photo/address ID proofs (self-attested).
