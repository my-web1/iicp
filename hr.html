IICP's HR & People Management Specializations

Flexible post-graduate diplomas and advanced certifications at IICP equip early and mid-career professionals with targeted in-depth expertise for HR, IR, Labour Laws, and modern people management. All programs are available in fast-track and regular modes. Typical duration is 3–12 months, with flexible schedules to suit working professionals. 

Benefits

  • Industry-oriented, practical curriculum crafted by HR and legal experts
  • Flexible study options (online, fast-track, or weekend/rush mode)
  • Affordable fees with access to IICP learning resources
  • Built for upskilling, career progression, or domain specialization
  • Certification enhances CV for HR, IR, compliance, or L&D roles

PGDLL – Labour Laws  (Fees: ₹10,500.)

  • Program Overview: In-depth legal framework for employment, social security, and industrial dispute resolution.
  • Ideal for: HR, compliance, and legal professionals.
  • Core Topics: Industrial Disputes, Trade Union Law, Labour Welfare Legislation, Social Security.

PGDTND – Training & Development (Fees: ₹ 10,500.)

  • Program Overview: Practical skills in designing, delivering, and assessing impactful learning and development solutions.
  • Ideal for: L&D, HR, and training professionals.
  • Core Topics: Talent Development, ADDIE Model, Learning Technologies, Performance Support.

PGDS – Industrial Psychology (Fees: ₹ 10,500.)

  • Program Overview: Psychological principles in workplace behaviour, motivation, and performance.
  • Ideal for: HR, managers, psychology graduates.
  • Core Topics: Work Motivation, Organizational Growth, Performance Management, Leadership Behaviour.

PGDIR – Industrial Relations (Fees: ₹ 10,500.)

  • Program Overview: Manage industrial relations, conflict resolution, and compliance in modern workplaces.
  • Ideal for: HR managers, IR officers, compliance professionals.
  • Core Topics: Industrial Relations, Labour Laws, Negotiation Skills, Employee Welfare.

PGDHRM – Human Resource Management (Fees: ₹ 10,500.)

  • Program Overview: Comprehensive coverage of modern HR practices and strategy.
  • Ideal for: Aspiring or current HR professionals/managers.
  • Core Topics: HRM Principles, Talent Management, Labor Law, Organizational Behaviour, HR Analytics.

Advance Certification – HR Analytics  (Fees: ₹7,500.)

  • Program Overview: Analytical skills for data-based HR decisions and business impact.
  • Ideal for: HR professionals, business analysts
  • Core Topics: Metrics, Dashboards, Predictive Modeling, People Data.

Advance Certification – Employee Engagement (Fees: ₹7,500.)

  • Program Overview: Best practices to drive motivation, retention, and positive culture.
  • Ideal for: HR, L&D, and people managers.
  • Core Topics: Engagement Metrics, Recognition, Communication, Change Management.

Advance Certification – Compensation & Benefits (Fees: ₹7,500.)

  • Program Overview: Build/assess compensation programs and total rewards strategy.
  • Ideal for: HR, payroll, compensation analysts.
  • Core Topics: Salary Structures, Incentives, Benefits, Compliance.

Executive Diploma in HRM (Fees: ₹ 7,500.)

  • Program Overview: Advanced HR management for emerging leaders and career switchers.
  • Ideal for: Managers and experienced HR professionals.
  • Core Topics: Strategic HRM, Leadership, Analytics, Talent & Workforce Planning.

Advance Certification – HR Leadership & Strategy (Fees: ₹ 7,500.)

  • Program Overview: Strategic people leadership and business alignment for HR leaders.
  • Ideal for: HR managers, business partners, consultants.
  • Core Topics: HR Strategy, Change Management, Succession Planning, Ethics.

Advance Certification – Performance Management (Fees: ₹ 7,500.)

  • Program Overview: Best practices in KPIs, appraisal, and feedback for organizational growth.
  • Ideal for: Managers, HR, L&D, and business leads.
  • Core Topics: Performance Frameworks, Goal Setting (OKRs), Coaching, Rewards.

All programs: Flexible durations (3–12 months), fees ₹7,500, online/fast-track options, with certification support for career growth in HR and related fields.

IICP is an autonomous institute accredited by the Global Distance Learning Association (GDLA), Sydney, Australia. All programs are independently developed to meet industry needs and do not involve the University Grants Commission (UGC) or All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Our certificates are specifically designed for professionals seeking practical, globally-relevant skills, and are respected across the private sector.
