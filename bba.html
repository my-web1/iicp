IICP's Bachelor Program in Business Administration (BBA)

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) at the International Institute of Career Progression (IICP) is a comprehensive undergraduate program designed for students aiming to build a strong foundation in business, management, and leadership. This program blends theoretical learning with practical skills, preparing graduates for entry-level positions, entrepreneurial ventures, or further management studies.

Why Choose IICP’s BBA?

  • Comprehensive Business Education: Core knowledge across management, marketing, HR, finance, and operations.
  • Career-Oriented Curriculum: Industry-relevant content and skill-building workshops.
  • Specialization Pathways: Choose electives to tailor your learning (e.g., Marketing, HR, Finance, Entrepreneurship).
  • Flexible Learning: Online/regular and fast-track study options for accessibility and speed.
  • Affordable Fees: Designed to maximize access for Indian and international students.
  • Placement & Progression Support: Guidance for internships, jobs, and higher education.

Eligibility

  • Educational Qualification: 10+2 (Higher Secondary) or equivalent in any stream.
  • Who Should Apply:
    • School leavers interested in business and leadership roles.
    • Entrepreneurial students planning to start their own ventures.
    • Students seeking a pathway to MBA or other postgraduate management studies.
    • Anyone aiming to join family businesses or management roles across industries.

Key Features

  • Strong Foundation: Develops business acumen and leadership skills from day one.
  • Industry Integration: Project work, case studies, and guest lectures from business leaders.
  • Career Support: Internship & placement guidance, mentorship from experienced faculty.
  • Mobility: Smooth transition to IICP’s MBA or other recognized postgraduate programs.

Admission Process

  1. Review eligibility and select BBA program mode (fast-track or regular/online).
  2. Submit application form and educational documents (10+2 certificate, photos, ID proof).
  3. Consult with IICP advisors if needed for specialization/career guidance.
  4. Complete admission and begin your learning journey.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is work experience required to enter the BBA?
No. The BBA is designed for recent school graduates and early aspirants.
Can I pursue the BBA while working part-time?
Yes. Flexible schedules and online resources make it possible to balance work and study.
Does the BBA support direct admission to an MBA?
Yes. Successful BBA graduates get preferred entry to IICP's MBA program (and it is recognized by most institutions for postgraduate applications).
What document are required for admission?
10+2 certificate (mark sheet), 3 passport photographs, and ID proof (all self-attested).
