Accredition and quality 

The International Institute of Career Progression (IICP)

IICP is globally recognized for delivering quality-driven education and career solutions. Certified for ISO 9001:2015 by AssureMark International and accredited by the Global Distance Learning Association (GDLA), IICP demonstrates a commitment to continuous improvement, learner satisfaction, and best practices in academic administration.

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

IICP has been certified by AssureMark International for ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management System), reaffirming its dedication to delivering accountable and consistent education services.

View Certificate

GDLA Accreditation

Recognition from the Global Distance Learning Association affirms IICP’s quality, transparency, and learner-first philosophy, positioning it as a trusted institution for working professionals and aspiring learners worldwide.

Explore Accreditation

IICP is an autonomous institute accredited by the Global Distance Learning Association (GDLA), Sydney, Australia. All programs are independently developed to meet industry needs and do not involve the University Grants Commission (UGC) or All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Our certificates are specifically designed for professionals seeking practical, globally-relevant skills, and are respected across the private sector.
