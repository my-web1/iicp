Need Assistance?

Have more questions? Our support team is here to help you at every step of your learning journey with IICP.

Connect with Support

What is IICP?

IICP is an autonomous institute offering flexible, industry-focused management and IT education through online and distance learning for working professionals and graduates.

What support does IICP provide?

Comprehensive support includes study notes, e-content, online video references, assignments, assessments, and 24/7 access to an online portal for queries and resources.

Are IICP programs recognized internationally?

Certificates are valued by private sector employers in India and abroad, though recognition depends on local regulations.

Is classroom attendance required?

No. Programs are self-paced via online or distance learning, with optional contact classes.

What is distance education at IICP?

Study remotely with online materials, video references, and assignments—ideal for working professionals.

Does IICP provide study materials?

Yes, materials are sent via courier or accessible via the student portal, email, or WhatsApp.

Is IICP accredited by AICTE/UGC?

IICP operates as an autonomous institute and is not governed by AICTE or UGC, as these bodies regulate universities and full-time degree programs, not independent professional institutes. IICP awards industry-recognized certificates upon successful course completion, emphasizing practical skills and real-world application. These certifications are highly valued in the private sector for their relevance and adaptability to current industry needs.

How many branches does IICP have?

IICP is an online institute with globally accessible resources and support available from anywhere. It operates primarily online, with physical coordination from Pune-PCMC.

Can I take exams from home?

Yes, many exams are open-book, emphasizing case studies and practical application.

Why doesn’t IICP advertise in print media?

We prioritize student support and career enhancement.

How do I apply for IICP courses?

Apply online or submit a completed form with payment. Admission is direct for eligible candidates.

What are the examination modes?

Exams are case-study, email, or center-based, held quarterly (March, June, September, December).

How are study materials distributed?

Sent via email and WhatsApp to your registered email address and phone number or accessible online.

What is the passing percentage?

Overall passing percentage is 50%.

When can I take exams?

Quarterly exams, with early exam options via a pre-exam fee.

Can I take exams before 6 months?

Yes, with a pre-examination fee.

Can I pursue extra specializations?

Yes, with an additional fee per specialization.

When are exam results declared?

Results and certificates are issued within 7-10 working days post-submission.

When will I receive study materials?

Within 4-7 working days of enrollment.

Can I complete an MBA in 6 months?

Yes, via the fast-track option, based on prior experience.

Are fast-track MBAs accepted by companies?

Yes, certificates don’t mention duration and are valued by reputed organizations.

Is IICP’s MBA equivalent to a university MBA?

Comparable in industry recognition and curriculum but awarded as a professional diploma/certificate.

Are IICP programs recognized for government jobs?

Many alumni secure government jobs, but some roles may require UGC/AICTE degrees. Check job requirements.

How do I get a duplicate ID card?

Request a duplicate ID card for a fee of Rs. 250.

How many MBA specializations does IICP offer?

Over 70 management specializations tailored to diverse career goals.

Can I change my specialization?

Yes, with a request and applicable fee.

How can I pay for my course?

Pay online. Installment options are available, and fee receipts are provided.

Are there additional charges beyond the course fee?

No hidden fees. Course fees cover study materials, registration, and exams. Extra charges apply for fast-track, re-exams, or lost documents.

Is the course fee refundable?

Fees are non-refundable and non-adjustable as per IICP policy.

Will I receive a fee receipt?

Yes, receipts are issued for all payments, including installments.

Does IICP provide degrees or certificates?

IICP awards certificates, valued for practical and industry relevance.

What are short-term/modular courses?

Accelerated programs for experienced learners, completable in less than standard duration.

What are the benefits of IICP’s autonomous status?

Updated curricula, flexible durations, diverse specializations, and a focus on employability.

What is certification, and why is it valuable?

IICP certifications meet industry standards, enhancing career prospects.
