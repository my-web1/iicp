IICP's Diploma in Business Administration

Program Overview

The Diploma in Business Administration at the International Institute of Career Progression (IICP) is a foundation program crafted for students and working professionals seeking core business knowledge and practical managerial skills. It enables learners to build competence in business fundamentals and prepares them for administrative, supervisory, or support roles across sectors.

Why Choose IICP’s Diploma in Business Administration?

  • Core Business Knowledge: Covers fundamental concepts in management, marketing, finance, and HR.
  • Practical Skills: Focused on real-world business challenges and essential administrative processes.
  • Flexible Study Modes: Available as fast-track (6 months), regular (1 year), and online formats for all schedules.
  • Affordable Fees: Accessible cost for Indian and international students.
  • Career Progression: Ideal pathway for entry-level jobs or further higher studies in business administration.

Eligibility

  • Educational Qualification: 10+2 (Higher Secondary) or equivalent in any stream.
  • Who Should Apply:
    • School graduates seeking a business foundation.
    • Professionals aiming for basic management and supervisory skills.
    • Anyone interested in progressing to advanced business/management studies.

Key Features

  • Industry-Ready: Builds job-oriented administrative/office management skills from day one.
  • Flexible Duration: Suited to both students and working professionals.
  • Affordable Route: Entry-level business qualification for career starters.
  • Progression Opportunity: Enables direct pathway to BBA or MBA for eligible candidates.

Admission Process

  1. Check eligibility and select program mode (fast-track or regular/online).
  2. Submit application with 10+2 certificate, 3 passport photographs, and ID proof.
  3. Confirm admission with IICP’s guidance team and begin learning.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need work experience to enroll?
No, this program is open to both freshers and working individuals.
Can the diploma be pursued alongside a job or further studies?
Yes. Study modes allow for flexible schedules and online access.
Is this diploma recognized by employers?
Yes. It is a valuable credential for basic administrative, support, and entry-level business positions.
Can I move to BBA/MBA after this diploma?
Yes, eligible diploma holders can progress to advanced business degree programs at IICP or other institutions.
