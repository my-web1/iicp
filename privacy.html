Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how IICP collects, uses, processes, and discloses your information, including personal information, in conjunction with your access to and use of our services.

1. Introduction

When you use IICP’s services, you trust us with your information. We take this responsibility seriously and are committed to protecting your information while giving you control over it.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can access, update, or delete your information.

2. Information We Collect

IICP collects information to provide and improve our educational services. The types of information we collect include:

2.1 Information You Provide

When you submit an admission form or enroll in a course, you provide us with personal information that includes:

  • Your first name and last name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Selected course

2.2 Automatic Information

We may automatically collect limited information when you use our website:

  • Browser type and settings
  • Log information (e.g., pages visited, time spent)

3. How We Use Your Information

IICP uses the information we collect to deliver, maintain, and enhance our educational services. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Process your course enrollment
  • Send notifications and updates about your course
  • Contact you via email or phone regarding course details and related services
  • Improve our website and services
  • Ensure the security of our services

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

IICP does not share your personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of IICP except in the following cases:

4.1 With Your Consent

We share personal information with third parties, such as FormSubmit.io for processing admission forms, when you consent to do so by submitting the form.

4.2 For Legal Reasons

We will share personal information if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation, or disclosure is reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests
  • Enforce IICP’s Terms of Service
  • Detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of IICP, our users, or the public

5. Data Security

IICP is committed to protecting your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We implement measures such as:

  • Using SSL encryption for our website
  • Regularly reviewing our data collection and storage practices
  • Restricting access to personal information to authorized IICP personnel only

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing your personal information
  • Correcting inaccurate information
  • Requesting deletion of your information
  • Opting out of notifications or calls by emailing info@iicp.in

7. Changes to This Policy

IICP may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the effective date. We may also inform you via email or phone, as per your consent.

Your continued use of IICP’s services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
